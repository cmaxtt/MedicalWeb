About Us

Welcome to Golden Care Medical Clinic, your premier healthcare provider in Arima. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional medical services with a patient-first approach. Our modern facility at the Shops of Arima is equipped with advanced diagnostic technology and staffed by a team of compassionate, highly-skilled professionals committed to your well-being.

  • Comprehensive Diagnostics

    State-of-the-art laboratory and imaging services including CT scans, X-rays, and Ultrasounds for accurate and timely diagnoses.

  • Specialized Care

    Access to a diverse network of medical specialists and experienced General Practitioners under one roof.

  • Patient-Centered Treatment

    Personalized care plans tailored to individual needs, ensuring the best possible health outcomes for every patient.
